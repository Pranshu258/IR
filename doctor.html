Improving the Emacs Doctor

This is an improved version of the "emacs doctor". A few more functionalities have been added to the original version that was distributed with "Emacs 24.5".



A PLAUSIBLE INTERACTION

Hi
How do you do? What brings you to see me?
I wanted to talk to you.
Why do you say you wanted to talk to me?
Because I am feeling bad.
Is it because you are feeling bad that you came to me?
Yes, can you fix me.
Earlier you said you are feeling bad?
Can you fix me is the question
Maybe your life has something to do with this.
Yes, I feel have a huge workload, may be that is the reason
Does it bother you that is the reason?
No, I must do the work
Is it because of your plans that you say you must do the work?
Yes, but I don't think that they are working
When did you first know that you do not think that they are working?
I came to know that last month
Can you elaborate on that?
Well, I was unable to finish the work before dedline
Perhaps the people you hang around with have something to do with this.
I think so, some of them are quite disturbing
What makes you believe you think so some them are quite disturbing?
Because, they are dirtbags
Does the fact that they are dirtbags explain anything else?
No, I think I need some rest, I shoul go now
I don't understand.
Bye, talk to you later
My secretary will send you a bill.
Ok, thanks

A NEGATIVE INTERACTION

Hi
How do you do? What brings you to see me?
I just wanted to talk to you
Why do you say you just wanted to talk to me?
Because I am sick
Is it because you are sick that you came to me?
Yes
I see... Well, what makes you believe this is so?
Because you are a doctor
Does it bother you that I am a doctor?
NO
Why not?
I think you are mad
Are you mad often?
I am not mad, you are mad
What causes you to be mad?
You are stupid
Earlier you said you are sick?
Good bye
My secretary will send you a bill.
I won't pay



So, as we can see, there are many possibilities where the conversation can go wrong. This was one example.
There are huge possibilities of improvement here.




LIST OF MODIFICATIONS


  • Changelog 1
    1. Added a function that invokes "Emacs Web Wowser" to search "Wikipedia" if the users ask for info about something.
    2. Added the ability to tell jokes when asked to do so.
    3. Added the ability to respond correctly if the users refuses to pay the doctor's bill.
    4. Added some more responses to qlist.
    5. Added the ability to tell its name when asked to do so.
  • Changelog 2
    1. Added responses for Thanks

Check out the source file: doctor.el
