WHAT IS RENJU ? Click here

RENJU is a professional variant of GOMOKU, a strategy board game originating in Japan. It is a two player game, the players take turns, placing moves on the board. A player wins the game when he/she gets 5 of his/her own stones in a row (the row can be in any direction).
